How to Use

  1. Enter the tray's internal dimensions (length, width, height), material thickness, and how many compartments you want.
  2. Click "Generate Drawing" to see the panel layout in the preview.
  3. Download as SVG or DXF and import into your laser cutting software (LightBurn, CorelDraw, Illustrator, etc.).
  4. The outer shell (Bottom, Front, Back, Left, Right) is fully finger-jointed, assemble that first. The bottom panel has a slot for each divider, and each divider is cut slightly taller so its extra length forms a tab. Once the shell is assembled, slide each divider down from the top into its slot — no glue needed.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do the dividers get assembled?

The outer shell is fully finger-jointed — assemble that first. The bottom panel has a slot for each divider, and the divider panels are cut slightly taller so a tab passes through that slot. Slide each divider down from the top into its slot — it locks in place, no glue needed.

How many compartments can I choose?

Any number you like — compartments are automatically spaced evenly along the length.

Do the dividers need glue?

No — the tab on each divider locks into its slot in the bottom panel, so a snug fit holds it without glue.
