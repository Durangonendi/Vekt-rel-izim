Frequently Asked Questions

What is a finger joint box?

A laser-cut box style where small interlocking tabs and slots along each panel's edges lock together without glue. The zigzag pattern creates a tight, durable joint.

Which materials can I use?

MDF, plywood, acrylic, cardboard, and any other flat sheet material that can be laser cut. Just enter the actual thickness of your material.

Why does kerf matter?

The laser beam vaporizes a thin strip of material as it cuts, which makes parts come out slightly narrower than designed. Entering your kerf value compensates for this so the joints fit snugly.

Should I download SVG or DXF?

Most laser cutting software (LightBurn, CorelDraw, Illustrator, AutoCAD) can open either. DXF is more common in CNC/CAD software; SVG works more easily in web-based tools. If unsure, try both.
